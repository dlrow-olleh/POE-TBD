Why did we do this?

Initially, we wanted to create a Period Simulator, but due to the fact that there were too many constraints for that particular project we decided to sit down and come up with a set of goals that we would like our final project to execute. As we created them, each individual team member shared a few things in common, we were all passionate about promoting Self-care and wanted to create a product "we were proud of" and "that it would add value to someone's life". Our Drawing Bot, _______, ensures that individuals take time to themselves by sitting down, taking a deep breath, and appreciating the art that is created by the bot, live.

Our Goals

As stated earlier, we wanted to create something amazing and that would create some sort of impact. In order to achieve this, we broke down our main goals into smaller, specific goals that would allow us to create an amazing robot that would satisfy our larger goals.
